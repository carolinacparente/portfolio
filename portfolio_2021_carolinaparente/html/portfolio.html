PASSIONS AND DREAMS

Hey, I'm Carolina. Nice to meet you!

Design and Multimedia Graduate in ESAD, Caldas da Rainha with good team skills, creative mindset, and openness to improve. During my academic journey, I learned to work with technologies like Cinema 4D, Adobe After Effects, Adobe Premiere, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop, Figma, among others, and skills like Processing, 2D and 3D animation, 3D modeling, UX/UI, motion design, photography and video editing, and more. I am currently taking a Master's degree in Multimedia Design, Coimbra University, where I hope to develop my skill set.
Passionate since forever for the animation world and convinced that my professional career would have to be associated with Design and Multimedia, I tried to stay connected to the artistic world through the years in an academic career but also my personal life. Enrolling in these courses was a dream come true it has brought me new passions and hobbies.
